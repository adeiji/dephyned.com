About Graffiti

Graffiti is an iOS application which allows a user to "tag", or save, a location with a photo, text, or both, and share that "tag" with others. It's a simple concept really, but the uses cases are endless!

  • Go some place that you really like and you want to make sure that you don't forget it. Just tag it with a photo.
  • You go to another city in your country or another and you find a restaurant that's amazing. Or maybe you find a place that's simply beautiful. You want your friends, if they ever go there, to see that spot. "Tag it", and all your friends will automatically see that spot you "tagged" when they're in that city!
  • You have friends in town but places to take them just aren't coming to your mind. Well as soon as they follow you, when they get to your city, all those spots you like will pop up on their phone.
  • You see a sunset so beautiful that it inspires you to write a poem. Tag that spot with a poem and photo, and everyone who goes to that spot can read that poem and see that sunset!
  • You find a romantic spot that you feel you would love to take your girlfriend or boyfriend to, and you don't want to forget about it. Tag it, then at the click of a button get directions to that spot when the time is right!
  • You're going to a city you've never been to before and you want to know where to go. Follow someone from that city with similar interests and go to all their favorite spots.

As you can see, the possibilities are endless. So don't hesitate, sign up now! We only have a limited amount of spots available, so don't delay as they will fill up quickly.
